Miguel

A 12-year-old Mexican boy who lives in a lively, noisy Mexican village, has dreamed of music since he was a child. Yet he was born into a family of shoemakers, the Rivera, the only family in town who hated music. While secretly pursuing his musical dream, Mig accidentally enters the Land of the Dead, where he meets the spirits of his family.
